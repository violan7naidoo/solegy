Mission & Vission

To provide unparalleled professional, dependable and workmanship guaranteed service to all clients. To ensure that our fees are comparable to the service we provide. To provide quality products coupled with quality workmanship.

Overview

Solgey co. is a proudly owned South African company based along the North Coast, KwaZulu- Natal. Solgey co. has a Level 1 BBBEE rating and are 100% Black owned. Our team has over 24 years of experience specialiazing in solar water heating systems, heat pumps and plumbing services both in the domestic and commercial sectors. Our commitment in ensuring all projects are expertly completed have won our client’s commendation and recommendation throughout the years. Whilst our work speaks for itself our greatest strengths and success comes from our in-depth experience and understanding our clients individual needs.
